A little about me ✦

interior designer in the making

I am an interior design student with a passion for designing warm, soulful, and meaningful spaces. While my interest in design encompasses its aesthetic aspects, it is much more than that. My fascination lies in the ability of a designed space to affect the moods and behaviors of individuals.

In terms of style, my designs have a modern aesthetic quality along with a touch of culture from my native Sri Lanka. In addition to aesthetics, my designs incorporate a sense of warmth, atmosphere, and a story, enabling me to communicate through them.

My inspirations include small, cozy coffee shops, gentle lighting, natural materials, and homely settings. I find it interesting how details in the form of materials, lighting, and planning could transform an ordinary place into a memorable one.

My design style combines modern aesthetics with a cultural touch of Sri Lanka.

i love cozy cafes, soft lighting, and spaces that feels like home ♡

design is not just visuals,
it's how a space makes you feel.
